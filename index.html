What's going on in San Angelo?

Concho Valley Events is a non-profit organization that host events and festivals in and around the Concho Valley. We love to promote small and local businesses while creating awareness of the different art, cultures and culinary scene in San Angelo and the surrounding towns. Our three most popular events are Paintbrush Alley Market Days, Food Truck Festival and Off The Menu, Food + Wine Festival. We are currently working on a Summer Soul Fest featuring local blues artist and soul food and partnering with a local non-profit on a Blue Grass Concert.

Paint Brush Alley

Where:

Paintbrush Alley Market Days was established in the Spring of 2021, featuring local artist, small businesses, craftsmanship, homemakers, food vendors and more. Paintbrush Alley Market Days blossomed a beautiful relationship with Art in Uncommon Places. Together, we have been able to create some amazing events with the local art of Downtown San Angelo as the focal point. With each event, there are over 60 vendors in attendance.

San Angelo Food + Wine Festival

Where:

We’ll pour wines and craft beers from near and far to pair perfectly with food from the best West Texas chefs, restaurateurs, artisan producers, and crafters. You don’t want to miss this signature event. Your ticket covers tastings of the food and beverages. Come and enjoy a great night with great food, booze, and music!

San Angelo Food Truck Festival

Where:

The San Angelo Annual Food Trucks Festival is a family-friendly event that takes place at the Lake View Bible Church in San Angelo, Texas. The festival is focused on local food trucks and aims to provide an opportunity for people to enjoy some quality family time, live music and activities while sampling great local food. The event is sponsored to keep costs minimal for attendees, and there will be door prizes given out all day long, as well as a mobile adoption from "Critter Shack Rescue" for those looking to adopt or foster a dog in need. Free parking is available at the San Angelo Rodeo Association across the street at the Spur Arena.
