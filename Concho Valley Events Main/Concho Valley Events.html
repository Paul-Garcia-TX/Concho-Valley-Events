What's going on in San Angelo?

Concho Valley Events is a non-profit organization that host events and festivals in and around the Concho Valley. We love to promote small and local businesses while creating awareness of the different art, cultures and culinary scene in San Angelo and the surrounding towns. Our three most popular events are Paintbrush Alley Market Days, Food Truck Festival and Off The Menu, Food + Wine Festival. We are currently working on a Summer Soul Fest featuring local blues artist and soul food and partnering with a local non-profit on a Blue Grass Concert.

Who are we?

We are a non-profit social enterprise that believes in providing innovative communtiy opportunities that supports local small businesses. Our goal is to promote cultural awareness & education while supporting small businesses.
